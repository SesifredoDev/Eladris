## 📖 Backstory & Role in the Story

> The Ancient Silver Dragon Syarthdarakasyn served as the guardian of the Darakas Tribe. When Tegom-Draggorath attacked, he battled the red dragon for days before finally killing him, though he himself later died of his wounds.
> 
> Before dying, he pierced his own heart and bound his essence to the fragment of Tegom-Draggorath that would incarnate within the tribe, hoping to counter its corrupting influence.
