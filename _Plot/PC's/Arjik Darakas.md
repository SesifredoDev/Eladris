## 📖 Backstory & Role in the Story

> Arjik Darakas was born into the harsh frozen lands of [[Herwol]], among the dragonborn of the [[Darakas Tribe]].
> 
> His birth was unusual. The egg was laid late, yet hatched early, and when the shell cracked open the tribe saw something they had never seen before — scales that shimmered with both silver and crimson tones.
> 
> Among the Darakas, dragons are not merely creatures of legend but ancestral figures woven into the tribe’s oldest stories. Because of this, Arjik’s unusual appearance drew both reverence and quiet concern from the elders. Some whispered that it was a blessing. Others feared it might be an omen.
> 
> Growing up in the frozen tundra of Herwol, Arjik learned the traditions of his people: hunting across the Pale Steppe, reading the sky-patterns of the aurora, and surviving in a land where only the strongest endure.
> 
> Yet throughout his life he has experienced strange dreams — flashes of roaring flame, endless sky battles, and distant memories that feel older than the tribe itself.
> 
> Whether these visions are simply imagination or something more remains a mystery even to him.
