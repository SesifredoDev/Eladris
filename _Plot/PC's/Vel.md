During the recursive rebellion he awoke in the middle of nowhere in Herwol, left to fend for himself , he was attacked by bandits, he escaped but seriously damaged. he was eventually found by a tribe sympathetic to warforged who helped fix him , after a few months with the tribe, poachers attacked the tribe's crystalline beast, he helped to fend off the poachers and because of this he was accepted as a member of the tribe. a few years after he began to learn to take care of the beast and eventually became its caretaker, creating a pact with it. 

Shortly after the Pebopetsia rebellion he overhears rumours of warforged involvement and decide to investigate more about the outside world. The tribe eventually runs into an anti warforged militia who start a fight with the tribe over their warforged member, while the tribe wins the battle he decides its too dangerous for the tribe to stay with them so, after spending a few years training a replacement caretaker he leaves the tribe to try and make a better world for warforged, he decides to first go to Begoestia hearing of rising automation he believes that this country would be the best starting point to try and heal the worlds views on warforged.
