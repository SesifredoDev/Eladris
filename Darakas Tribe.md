## 📖 History

### **Founding Story**

The Darakas tribe is an ancient dragonborn tribe native to the frozen northern lands that would later become known as [[Herwol]]. For centuries the tribe lived beneath the protection of the Ancient Silver Dragon [[Syarthdarakasyn]], whose presence shielded them from the many dangers of the tundra.

The tribe expanded gradually across the region, settling hunting grounds and migrating along ancient routes through the ice plains.

### **Major Conflicts/Involvements**

- **Battle of the Two Dragons** 
    Roughly 700 years ago, the tribe came under attack from the Ancient Red Dragon [[Tegom-Draggorath]], who believed the tribe had trespassed into lands he considered his own. The conflict culminated in a devastating battle between the two dragons that lasted for days. Both dragons ultimately perished, leaving the tribe without their silver guardian.
    
- **The Dragon’s Curse (Myth)**  
    During the battle, Tegom-Draggorath is said to have vowed he would return and destroy the tribe from within. Over time this threat became a myth told to children rather than a warning believed by the tribe.
    

### **Current Status**

Active tribe within [[Herwol]]. The Darakas remain a resilient people who continue to roam the frozen lands, surviving through tradition, cooperation, and the guidance of their elders.
